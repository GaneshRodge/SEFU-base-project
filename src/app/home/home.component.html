SCFU Application Reference

General Dealer Financing (GDF) is an application that caters to the Dealer financing requirements of Industry majors, Dealers and Sub Dealers who are clients of SBI. The application is customizable for all customers who require a Dealer Discount Scheme and enjoy Dealer financing limits with State Bank of India.

General Vendor Financing (GDF) is an application that caters to the Vendor financing requirements of Industry majors, Vendors and Sub Vendors who are clients of SBI. The application is customizable for all customers who require a Vendor Discount Scheme and enjoy Vendor financing limits with State Bank of India.
